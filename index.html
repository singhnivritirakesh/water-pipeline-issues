About

Many areas face water supply issues due to ageing pipelines. These problems include delays, leakage, low pressure, and dirty water. By collecting data and complaints, we aim to improve water supply quality and reliability.

Common Problems

  • Low water pressure
  • Delayed water supply
  • Dirty or muddy water
  • Leakage and wastage
  • Frequent pipe bursts
  • Water contamination and bad smell
  • Inconvenience in daily life (cooking, cleaning, bathing)

How to File Complaint

  • Contact your local municipal office
  • Call water supply helpline
  • Use online complaint portal
  • Contact local representatives (councilor, MLA, etc.)
  • Share photos of leakage via our survey or Live Camera section

Suggested Solutions

  • Replace old pipelines with modern, durable materials
  • Regular maintenance and inspection of pipelines
  • Quick complaint resolution and tracking system
  • Better monitoring system (pressure sensors, leakage detection)
  • Public awareness and involvement via surveys and feedback
